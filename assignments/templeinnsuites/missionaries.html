Temple Missionaries

Serving as full-time temple missionaries? We are happy to have you! We gladly offer long-term accommodations in kitchenette suites at a discounted rate.

Our suites include all of the regular hotel amenities and comforts, such as free wi-fi for connecting with family at home, free breakfast daily, fitness center, indoor pool, and more.

Kitchenette

Save time and money by preparing and storing your food right in your room! The kitchenette in each suite offers a basic lineup of kitchenware and appliances, including:

  • Four place-settings of dishes (bowls, plates, glasses, mugs)
  • Four place-settings of silverware
  • Spatula, serving spoons, kitchen knives
  • Saucepan, frying pan, and large cooking pot
  • Baking sheet
  • Toaster and microwave
  • Refrigerator and freezer
  • Stovetop and oven
  • Small dining area

Laundry Services

All hotel-owned towels and bed linens for long-term suites are laundered every third day (more often on request). We offer dry-cleaning services for a small fee. For all other personal laundry, you will receive a keycard for our on-site laundry room. Laundry detergent not provided; however, a vending machine is available in the laundry room.
