Specialized Services for Temple Patrons

Temple History Lobby

Enjoy waiting in our beautiful lobby and sitting areas. Each has a temple history theme, showcasing the nearby temple we serve as well as famous Church temples.

Changing Rooms

Free access to changing rooms, day-use lockers and bathroom facilities is provided for temple patrons who have traveled far and need to change to and from church clothes.

Playroom

A playroom and low-cost, short-term child care is available for the children of temple patrons. Let the little ones enjoy crafts, toys, and activities with our energetic staff while you enjoy your temple visit without worry.

Family History Services

Every Temple Inn features a Family History Office, where you can receive a free family history consultation and print family ordinance cards before your temple visit. Appointments with our Family History Advisor can be requested at the front desk.
