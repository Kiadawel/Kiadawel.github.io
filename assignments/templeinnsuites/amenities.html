Featured Amenities

Complimentary Breakfast

Enjoy a free, complimentary breakfast in our dining room every morning. Our locations feature a range of choices, including but not limited to:

  • Custom Omelette Station (check your location for days and times)
  • Hot scrambled eggs and sausage
  • Make-your-own waffles station
  • Bagels with cream cheese
  • Muffins and Danishes
  • Cold beverages: juice, milk and ice water
  • Warming beverages: Herbal Tea and Hot Cocoa

Exercise Facilities

Raise your endorphins even higher with our bright, spacious facilities! Every Temple Inn has an exercise room, open 5am-10pm. Access requires hotel keycard. Adults and teens 14+ only, please. Equipment includes, but is not limited to:

  • Cardiovascular Equipment (Elliptical and Treadmill)
  • Free weights
  • TRX™ Suspension Trainer
  • Yoga/stretching area with floor mats

Indoor Pool

Enjoy a refreshing swim in our indoor pool any day between 7:00am and 10:00pm (adults and children 12+ only after 8pm). Please use our striped "swim towels," available by request at the front desk. Children under 12 must be supervised by an adult.

Free Wi-Fi

Stay connected to home during your stay with our free high-speed internet. All Temple Inn locations feature a stable Wi-fi connection from any room or suite in the hotel. If a quiet workplace is needed, the breakfast room is available after 11am daily.

Other Amenities

Our services are always changing to better serve you. We aim to make your stay as comfortable as possible. Other amenities that we offer include, but are not limited to:

  • Shuttles to/from the nearest temple
  • Convenient parking near the hotel
  • Cots and extra bedding available on request
  • Concierge service
